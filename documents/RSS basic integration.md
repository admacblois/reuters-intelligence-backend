THOMSON REUTERS

This document aims to explain how to access the Reuters RSS URLs.

# 1.

To make us of the Reuters RSS URLs, it is necessary to have a Reuters Connect account validated for RSS feeds. Don't hesitate to contact your Account Manager or Technical Support to request it. After your account has been validated, an email should be received with your RSS key. The RSS login corresponds to the Reuters Connect username.

# 2.

After your Reuters Connect account is enabled for RSS feeds, click over your username as shown in the image below, go to “My Settings” and scroll down to the RSS section.

# 3.

The RSS section should show the RSS URLs available based on the subscribed channels.

# 4.

Copy/Paste the RSS URLs into your RSS reader or integrator tool.

# 5.

To do particular searches within an RSS feed, an RSS API should be used. Ask our technical team for the RSS API technical documentation.

There are a variety of means by which you can contact Thomson Reuters for support.

One of the easiest is to send an email to ReutersMediaSupport@thomsonreuters.com, which a customer representative should contact you with within 15 minutes.
